Top Picks for the Best Sports Car in 2018

There are different reasons why sports car owners choose their own unique set of wheels. For instance, a driver might enjoy the throaty thrum of a powerful engine as though it is music to his ears. Possibly, most sports car owners fancy the nimble handling and swiftness of sports car which differentiates them from other luxury cars and sedans. Irrespective of what your expectation maybe regarding the best sports car, it is a sure bet that our list of the top 10 sports cars will lower your jaw like WOW!!!. Our choicest are gathered from sales record at CarMax between 1st January 2018 and 30th June 2018.

So, sit back and see why they are our top picks as we unfold the scroll!

10. BMW M3
  • Do you love a sporty sedan which is not just sporty but one furnished adequately with lots of luxurious features? If yes, you really need to check out BMW M3 2017 model. The four-door BMW M3 sports car is incredibly spacious compared to other conventional sports sedan. Besides its roomy interior, the BMW M3 remains sleeked and compact with its unique aerodynamic build.
  • For the year 2017 model, BMW M3 has undergone a series of an upgrade compared to previous years. The 2017 BMW M3 has only a single trim level although with varieties either as four or a five-passenger sleek sedan based on your personal choice. Features common to both options are; a navigation system, keyless entry/ignition, carbon-fiber trim accents, and heated 10-way front seats.
  • Also are various add-ons packages available such as; the competition package – designed to boost the engine’s performance and output by about 19HP and raises the size of the tires from 18-20inches. As an addition to this package is sports seats, sleek chrome tailpipes and adjustable driving modes which gives an excellent experience while driving. So, if you need a sports car of high brawn and not limited in space, the 2017 BMW M3 will absolutely be the best choice for you.
9. Subaru BRZ
  • Introduced in 2013, Subaru BRZ experienced its most significant update last year. Though still with its two-door design, the Subaru BRZ even accommodates four back sitter who can seat very comfortably especially if they are kids. The upgrading tweaks were necessary to enable the coupe to fit perfectly with its performance package. A few of such package includes a wider Brembo® brakes which are designed to aid a faster-stopping power so that you can confidently hit the twisties.
  • There are about three trim levels to pick from with the 2017 Subaru BRZ. These include Series, Premium and Limited. With any trim level you choose, the rear-wheel-drive Subaru BRZ is embedded with a six-speed manual transmission and a 2.0L four-cylinder engine. Do you need plenty of responsive handling and power? This engine and speed combination is sure to do just that.
  • With a stickshift version of a stop, expect a 2016 Subaru BRZ to go as far as 60mph. However, the 2017 Subaru BRZ which is equipped with the fast six-speed manual transmission and a 2.0L four-cylinder engine, can go an approximate of 21mpg in the town and 29mpg on a highway (1).
8. Mazda MX-5 Miata
  • The “Retractable Fastback” design of MX-5 Miata was the new design to bless 2017. The retractable roof of the Mazda MX-5 Miata is pretty swift that it can retract before a stoplight changes from yellow to green. Adding to this is the low-slung physique and nice powerful lines. In case you don’t want the entire roof to stow away completely, just the overhead roof panel and the rear window can be hidden. The coupe is a cool ride for a two-seater.
  • The 2017 Mazda MX-5 Maita has three trim levels which are all powered by an average 2.0L four-cylinder engine capable of delivering about 155HP. The sports trim level of this car has a black cloth-trimmed sports seat while the club design has, also, a red accent edging. The Grand Touring trim level will blow your mind with its supple leather seats which you can comfortably in sink into.
7. Subaru WRX
  • The elemental compositions of the best sports car such as rear-wheel drive, two passengers with two doors are all the features the Subaru WRX carries all alone. Being able to accommodate five passengers, the 2017 Subaru WRX Premium is wrought by a turbocharged engine with four-cylinders. The nimble handling and six-speed stick shift are the bombs about the Subaru WRX.
  • The nimble handling and precise steering are the features that are retained in the WRX in 2017. In case of wheel spinning, while turning on slippery terrain, the all-wheel drive of the Subaru WRX will definitely give you more traction that will enable you to reduce power loss.
6. Nissan 370Z
  • Are you moved by a sports car with classic styling? Then the 2017 Nissan 370Z will surely catch your fancy. The convertible and coupe models of this car come with a standard V6 engine which is located far behind the car’s frame. Also, it has a well distributed and balanced weight that enhances its maneuverability. We believe you’ll love that.
  • Sports car traditionalist will always go for the 370Z’s embedded six-speed manual transmission. Need no stress? You can sit back while the seven-speed automatic carries out the shifting on your behalf. Do you need some classic race car looks? Choose the Nismo coupe-only model.
5. Volkswagen GTI
  • The Volkswagen Golf GTI was introduced into the US market in1983, and it has remained with high reputation since then. Despite its compact body, it is built with a powerful engine, sporty style, a generous cargo compartment and a four-door rear door. Towards the ending of 2016, two-door Volkswagen Golf GTIs were out of stock, and the new design comes in four trim levels.
  • The 2017 Volkswagen is a front-wheel-drive sports car that has a 2.0L four-cylinder turbocharged engine as its standard component. For a higher horsepower under the coverage of this portable powerhouse, autobahn trim level or Sports SE which can raise your HP to about 220 is what you should go for.
4. Chevrolet Corvette
  • Most American sports car lovers rarely go wrong with the Chevrolet Corvette. Either as a convertible or as a low-slung coupe, the two-seater sporty Chevrolet Corvette have your choice covered. Already in existence were the Stingray Z51, Stingray and Z06 trim level, but the Grand Sports trim level which comes in new in 2017 combines all these trim levels. Do you need a roof panel you can easily pull of and store in the trunk? The 2017 Chevrolet Corvette Stingray sports car is designed and built to give you this flexibility.
  • Also, a seven-speed manual transmission which is complemented with a V8 engine is what the 2017 Stringray base model built with. With so much attention to comfort, the 2017 Chevrolet Corvette’s snug is designed to give you the experience of the support of a royal sofa.
3. BMW X1
  • If what you need is a classy sports sedan that can accommodate your cargo with ample space for a passenger, then you need to have a glimpse of a small sports SUV like the 2017 BMW X1. Despite its luxurious and sporty style and features, the two-row crossover BMW X1 can still seat about five passengers conveniently.
  • For the record, xDrive28i AWD and sDrive28i FWD are the two trim levels of the 2017 BMW X1. Common to these trim levels are; Bluetooth connectivity, comfortable IDrive interface, rain sensitive wipers, and quality vinyl upholstery. Guess what. The interior of the X1 is perfect because it is not just designed to be beautiful, but it is also spacious. Do you have a list of a prospective sports car to go for?
  • Sincerely, the X1 might not make the first row, but it is worth being a part of the list especially as it doesn’t spare interior space.
2. Chevrolet Camaro
  • The breeze of a major overhauling blew over the Chevrolet Camaro in 2016, and since then the sleek coupe is rapidly evolving into a sports car of all times. Surprised to know that the 2017 Chevrolet Camaro SS can resonate from 0-60 within 4 seconds? Its 6.2L four-cylinder V8 engine gives it this potential.
  • Despite being powerful and swift, the Chevrolet Camaro moves responsively and precisely with its high-quality interior design and comfortable front seat design. Do you need a sports car that will always make your commute experience adept and nimble? The Chevrolet Camaro is one sports car ready to give you such experience.
1. Ford Mustang
  • Undisputedly, another sports car of all time that will always make the list of many American sports car lovers’ list is the Ford Mustang. With over 50 years of illustrious history about it, the Mustang presents various zip handing both as a perfect sports car for a weekend ride or as a commuter car.
  • •For Mustang lovers who love their, Mustang packed of adrenaline-charged and a bit noisy, the Ford Mustang GT350 which has been labeled a ‘future classic’ is entirely what you need to check out.
  • With its four-seaters, the Ford Mustang gives you the ease of moving the kids around and still grooving under the fun of a sports car that has pep and style.
  • The trim level such as the 2017 Ford Mustang GT has an inbuilt track app which automatically keeps track of your lap time as well as other information about your ride.
  • More so, with the rearview camera, reversing into some tight parking space can be a fun game.
