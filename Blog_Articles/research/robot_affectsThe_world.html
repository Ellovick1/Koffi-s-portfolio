How Will Robots Affect the World in the Future?

1.0 Introduction

The nexus between technology and our daily living, today and the future continue to evolve. The great innovative waves which accompanies it makes humans optimistic and at the same time, heightens fears (González Vázquez et al. 2019). Historically, automation often presents adantages and disadvantages. With current applications and wide usage, it is therefore necessary to examine its effects to the world and future. One of such technology is Robotics.

A Robot is an independent machine that is skilled in detecting its surroundings, performing computations by making a decision, and carrying out activities in real-time as the programme by a computer. From the above definition, it is clear that robots operates based on the programmable design. In other words, it operates according to” programmable by a computer”. It uses a set of sensors that enable it to sense its environment as well as performing a complex task. Robots are categorized according to the setting or environment, in which they operate. The most general forms of robots are, fixed and mobile robots and they operate in different working environments as well as requiring diverse capabilities. Fixed robots are generally used in an industrial environment because this set of robots can only carry out their task in well-defined settings. They carry out particular and repetitive tasks such as painting or soldering in a manufacturing plant (Georg and Guy Michaels, 2015).

With the recent advancement of devices and sensors for human-robot interaction, the robot can be employed in a supervised environment that requires high precision. Contrarily, mobile robots are usually seen everywhere performing one task or the other in both conventional and ill-defined environments. For decades now, the dramatic increase of the international standard of industrial robots and has been estimated to drastically increase shortly, owing to what is going on in China’s pace of setting up. Robotics manufacturing has witnessed radical investment progress, overturning decades-long drifts of continuing and balanced growth. A meeting of modernisms in digital pieces of knowledge such as machine learning and artificial intelligence alongside developments in robotics production and energy storage is radically converting the abilities of robots. The emergence of automation offers substantial prospects for businesses to increase productivity.
