The Impact of Artificial Intelligence on US Economic Growth

Abstract

The nexus between artificial intelligence and economic growth has received much attention in literature. Numerous studies stress that AI will have a major economic impact including major surges in labor productivity, efficient workforce with transferrable benefits to the economy through diffusion of innovation. However, others its destructive characteristics to growth. This study sought to confirm the economic growth Artificial intelligence nexus for the US economy. The study followed a time series analysis. The data for the US economy was sourced from World Data Indicator for the period (1990 to 2018). Preliminary investigation revealed that the variables are stationary at levels. The study examines the impacts of artificial intelligence on economic growth using the ordinary least squares regression technique. The result indicates the relationship between economic growth with all variables. Artificial Intelligence (n= 28, β= -0.21, p < 0.05), for Labor, it is (n= 28, β = -0.004, p < 0.05), Labor and artificial intelligence interaction (n= 28, β = 0.019, p < 0.05). Following this result, this study proposes upgrade in the skills and competencies of existing staff through training and re-training for mutuality between humans and machines. This proof to be the safety net in countering the negative shocks of artificial intelligence to the US economy.

Keywords: Artificial Intelligence, Ordinary Least Squares Regression, Labour, Economic Growth

Introduction

Artificial intelligence (AI) basically refers to the ability of a machine to behave and reason intelligently like humans. Certainly, since 1820, scholars have witnessed the emergence of many technologies which led to machine enhanced task. During the eighteen century, it was the invention of steam engine, later the combustion engine in the early twenties before the revolutionalization of information technology between 1970s and 1980s. Artificial intelligence moves just a step further to automate tasks including car driving, giving health advice and playing games. Numerous studies stress that AI will have a major economic impact. Accenture, a consulting forecasted that by 2035, annual global economic growth rates would double consequence on the emergence of AI.
