DJ Adverb's Biography

From the dazzling sunset of the Atlas Mountain to the colorful tanneries of Fez, now in Montreal, Canada is a musical legend and trailblazer by definition, well known for his rhythmic mix, DJ Abverb. Abverb is a first class rated DJ whose sublime debut dates back to 2006. Inspired by his father’s eclectic flare in Arab classic and talent of the traditional guitar; the aoud, Abverb became disposed to the old school music, learnt the ways of the traditional percussion and started his DJ career; “the old school” in 2010.

Young Abverb steadily settled into life as a DJ, scratching the old school disc joystick, relating to everyone within his reach and uniting the dance floor. With a mix of the melodic progressive and the tech-house instrumentals, DJ Abverb aligned his interest with 4 others to form an ethereal group called the Earonic, in 2016. His love and interest in old school classic launched him to the next level. Soon, he fashioned a niche known as “the afro-house music” a beautiful style from his old fanatics; the Arab classic.

As an icon and second to none, his musical selection flusters other DJ. Abverb now play the Afro-House Music to all people, electrifying the dance hall with his showmanship, and crowd commanding performance. Though he loves playing in parties, nightclubs, and special events, his love for music goes beyond just mixing tunes. DJ Abverb is also a producer who is set to develop his own crew of creativity. As a DJ and musical pundit, he has found plenty of ways to express himself. For him, it is all about the grooves; playing those tracks that gets you tapping your feet and nodding your head irrespective of your style. Abverb, a management graduate turn DJ, hope to storm the industry and tour the world with his artistic show in the nearest future.
