The Bonsai Master; A Tim West Autobiography

Tim West is a Maui base Bonsai legend. Though he is a Coloradan by Origin, his interest and appreciation for the art “Bonsai” won him more heart than the cartographic display of the Coloradans. Inspired by his early admiration in gardening and love for nature, Tim West grew fascinated by the alluring styles of the Bonsai trees and soon became disposed to the Bonsai art. His sublimed debut to the Bonsai art dates back 25years ago, during the 90s. With a convincing knowledge and well-articulated background in gardening, he learnt the way of the Bonsai, self-studied and developed an in-depth understanding of it.

As a young man who was quite passionate about learning new techniques and growing in the art, Tim visited the Bonsai master present at the time in the land of Smiles ‘Thailand” to further the art. He gained more knowledge, made new friends, and waxed stronger his proficiency. Just like the roots which sprout outwardly in its time, young Tim found plenty of ways to express his craftsmanship. He soon fashioned a niche for himself and settled for the unique designs of the Adenium, Desert Rose, Fiscus, and the Banyan Tree. Following his early success as a Bonsai pundit, he launched into offering guidance and tutelage to young minds interested in the Bonsai art.

As an icon and second to none, Tim's wonderful hobby creates happiness for many while his artistic values dazes other Bonsai masters. He is a former President to the Valley Isle Bonsai of Maui and has featured in many exhibitions to share his horticulturist experience, unite the Bonsai values and relates to everyone within his reach. At his leisure, Tim spent most of his time at his backyard with this Bonsai, a routine he tags “Zen.” For him, it is about the peace and happiness he derives during his Zen. As a Bonsai Master, Tim West, a Maui based Bonsai specialist, bases his craft on a tripartite principle of individuality, harmony, and elegance; a lesson he teaches to all.
