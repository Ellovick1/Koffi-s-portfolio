Executive Summary

The current report documents the findings from a survey involving a sample set of accommodation provider who have guests used their premises. The descriptive and inferential statistical analysis is used for the estimation. The report is structured in about four (4) sections as scheduled.


In section one, the measures of central tendency; mean, median and mode is used to estimate the continuous variables- number of beds and numbers of guest who used the premises of accommodation providers while measures of dispersion- range, interquartile range, standard deviation, and variance is used to analyze the continuous variable; revenue earned.


In section two, a description of the discrete probability distribution is done. A discrete random variable is identified from the survey and using an appropriate discrete probability distribution technique, a real time business problem is solved. Deduction from the survey data is made using the inferential statistics and documented in chapter three. In this chapter, the theoretical background to the normal (and sampling) distribution comprising the definition of the normal distribution, the essence of a normal distribution in sampling, the basis for inferential statistics and explanation of a continuous random variable is presented.


Having defined the confidence interval, a 95% confidence interval for a selected continuous variable i.e the number of guest visiting the premises of accommodation provider in the survey area is estimated and interpretation provided accordingly. In the final section which is four, a regression technique is used to examine the impacts of rating, number of beds, number of guests, number of guest taking breakfast, number of guest taking dinner, length of stay of current guest, and average time spent on the revenue of the facility owners.


The number of guest and number of guest taking breakfast significantly explained the variations in the revenue of accommodation providers in the survey. The test for one of the four assumptions of the linear regression is verified and the details and results of all analysis in this survey is properly documented and scheduled below.
