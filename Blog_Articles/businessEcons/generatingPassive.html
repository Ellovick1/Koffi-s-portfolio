All that you need is know where to kick off, then decide on what resource you can afford to invest if you want to see your passive income stream start flowing. Here are some top secrets to generating passive income for yourself.

Passive Real Estate

With a Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT), all your money is adequately invested in real estate. And since it is the REIT that takes ownership of the property, you have little or nothing to worry about who handles or shuttles the duties of the landlord.
When investing in REIT, you are sure to be receiving higher returns even more than bonds, bank investment and stocks will give you. Also, you may choose to sell your returns in a REIT at any time you choose – this makes it very flexible even than having full ownership of a real estate.
If you have difficulty in deciding which REIT will help you achieve your passive income dream, these few below can be the perfect spot to take off

Fundrise

Fundrise works best for people with little or no managerial skill, time or even upfront cost to buy a house, or if you wouldn’t want to be seen as a landlord. These guys can help you real good. All you need to do here is buy shares, and with your money invested in their shares, the money will be invested in developing a couple of real estate projects under their care. As these projects get completed and begin to boom, so do you. Unlike the traditional real estate deal which requires lots of money from your pocket, Fundrise gives you easy access into the real estate industry with as little as $500 which may even be less than your wardrobe allowance from your main job.

Furthermore, they have different investment strategies that will suit your passive income goal. Your goal may be a long term growth, balanced investing or even a supplemental income, no problem about that – there is always a plan to fit you.

Finally, Fundrise gives you an open cheque of investment where you choose from a list of their project which one you would want to invest in.

If you didn’t fancy the concept and offer that Fundrise brings to the table, but still need to invest in real estate with minimal risk, Realty Mogul might just be the guys you hope to work with.

Realty Mogul is a renowned private REIT that boasts of an average return of 8% for accredited investors. They bask on a unique 1031 exchange platform which permits an investor to sell their current real estate investment and reinvest their returns in a new property. With this, investors can evade tax payment only if they can complete the whole exchange process within six months (180 days). With tons of successful 1031 exchange recorded by Realty Mogul, they can confidently assure their investors of instant passive investment without Excrow, Closing Cost or Agents.

Vacation Rental (Rent Your Space)

You can as well earn massively from this great and quiet way of investing in real estate. Although this method is more of a hands-on method of investing in real estate all you need to do is rent out your vacation apartment, home or even a single room and watch the returns raise your account figures. Looks like this option makes more sense to you, right? You can check out these renowned firms to get your home listed.

Airbnb

This site gives people a better landing spot while traveling to various places around the world. With Airbnb, people travel to stay in wellfurnished apartments that are way less expensive than conventional hotels. With this website, you can enlist your home into three categories thus; entire home shared a home, or private room. This gives your guest a wide variety of options to choose from. How do you earn, right? For every booking to your home, Airbnb charges you a token sum of 3% of the total sum while your payment is remitted into your bank account 24hours after your guest check-in. Instead of leaving your home to sit empty, you can use this simple approach to get extra bucks flying into your bank account.

VRBO

There is still more to scoop! VRBO is another real property mogul that is known for legit home service for both owner and renters. With VRBO, all you need to do to start generating passive income for yourself is simply by listing your property. As soon as it has been given out to a guest, you start earning from wherever you are. Possibly, you may not want to get engaged in anything but simply sit and watch the return coming in, you can get more passive by outsourcing the management of your property to them. This might cause you a few bucks, though, but it is worth the investment.

Become a Silent Partner

Besides real property investment, another investment that is almost like a rival to real estate investment when it comes to generating passive income is becoming a silent partner. If you know of any successful business in need of additional funds to expand, you can become a sideline angel investor. Yes, no one knows you in relation to the business, yet you are earning from it.

Instead of investing your money in the form of loans to business proprietors as in the case of peer-to-peer lending, you can simply become a silent partner of businesses where you have an equity status in the business. While the business owner is solely responsible for the day-to-day supervision and management of the business, you only take the position of a silent contributor who participates in the proceeds of the business from the comfort of your home.

Sounds interesting, right? If yes, and you possibly don't know where to start from or what business to channel your investment tom AngelList can help you become an invisible equity holder of some great companies

With this, you can act as an angel investor to as many companies as your investment capacity can go, and in return, you enjoy 100% equity even while you are earning passively. What's more than this?
