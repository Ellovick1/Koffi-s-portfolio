SAVE TIME FOR YOUR SMALL BUSINESS BY GOING DIGITAL

Small Business does understand the verity which decision-making process entails and how daunting the process could also be. Hence, before money is committed to a project, pertinent questions are often answered including how the business can save money, how time would save and how returns on capital would be generated in the course of the operations. Business decisions which answer these questions stand a high chance of being successful. As the old saying reads, time is money. Wasted time is a wasted resource. Today, in the business world, this saying is more valid than ever. Our actions and in-actions that saves the business time are worth consideration. One way of saving time in today’s business is by going digital. In this digital age, small entrepreneurs and enterprises are innovatively implementing changes. With digitalization, a small business can leverage their scares and limited resources in more effective and smarter ways to provide efficiency, versatility and save time. The escalation and growth of smart devices and other digital tools create unique opportunities to improve the existing processes which can allow a business to save time. However, you would want to consider enhancing and saving business time by going digital. Here are some tips for you.

  • Use the Chatbots: Customer service operations and several other areas of businesses can be digitized. For instance, chatbots can be utilized to improve the automated messaging system. Chatbots are unique programmed robots or coded instructions that interface with clients and mimic human conversation through the use of artificial intelligence. For e-commerce firms, it interfaces with clients to simulate the shopping needs of buyers. Rather than have clients go through the rigor of filling out forms to acquire assistance, chatbots connect with clients and behave in ways that seem as though the clients are interacting with a retail customer associate. Some proprietary retail apps such as FreshDirect or Subway harbor several messaging bots. Seemingly, the Chatbots are a considerable way to structure your brand when the services provided are similar; both online and in-store. The client feels more comfortable on your webpage because their concerns and questions are answered almost immediately. In such a situation, the likelihood that such clients will leave your site for another competitor to find a solution or make an inquiry is low. This surges the speed and also improves the sales portfolio. If you’re considering one for your business, there are already numerous companies making use of the chatbots to improve and serve their clients better. Your business can acquire a unique custom chatbot, or utilize that of other companies which are into bot technologies to assist small businesses to link up their clients. Slackbot, facebook messengers, Alexa, and Kits are a few types of chatbots. The use of bots allows the company to offer services that are consistent, cohesive, and stable. This is because the responses from such a connection are controlled.
  • Use the Sales Force Automation Software: The crux of the sales performance management software is to enhance sales personnel plan and determine the order of dealing with leads to close out deals rapidly. With the sales performance management software, small business managers can outline deep insight which may pattern the general strategy and approach adopted for sales. Traditionally, the Sales performance software comprises commissions, incentives, quotas, goal setting, training, forecasting, and performance review. The sales performance software makes available the mechanism for improving these operations, allowing for a broad assessment of team and individual performance that management can utilize for planning, process optimization, and resource efficiency
  • Digitize your Human Resource operation: The use of software such as the SAAS-Base technologies is increasingly gathering momentum. Firms are digitizing their Human Resource role. Operations including managing employee benefits and entitlements, payroll activities or services, and other HR task are being managed digitally. This saves time, money and builds a Human Resource process that seeks to improve the core values of businesses. It promotes diversity and with its scalability factor, develops the team effectively. In addition to these mentioned roles, a digital Human Resource scheme is vital if small business desires to surge in today’s digital age. Task including training is now automated to allow the HR team to concentrate on issues which consume valuable human brain power. This saves a whole lot of time yet with increase productivity. So, wait no more. As a small business, seize the plunge and go digital. Scale up your operation, reduce cost and save time
  • Go digital and Step up your Accounting and Financial Processes: Digitalization can also be used in streamlining accounting and financial processes. For instance, the use of QuickBooks. QuickBooks is a small business management device or tool that provides payment, accounting, payroll, and time tracking services. These products are geared towards improving the lots of small or mini sized businesses including those who are self-employed. They possess an easy to utilize interface with numerous tutorials on how to maneuver the tool. To the new business, it serves as a complete valuable tool. Other financial activities can also be done digitally. For instance, the online invoicing services can lower the costs and time duration for which payment is received from clients. Online budgets could be tracked, assessed and analyzed, thereby reducing cost and saving time. Various or multiple sources of income can also be generated online.
  • Digitalize your operations, track project time and evaluate employees operation: Business thrives if tasks are properly and efficiently managed and when the team is kept accountable as well. Several tools are available for these operations. For instance; Google drive and Asana assist you to expend less time on planning and apportioning deadlines to projects thereby making ideas and work go simultaneously. More so, the sheet time tracking software; a holistic time management and tracking tool is a system that tracks and monitors the employee’s location remotely. With this tool, staff monitoring could be done without actually traveling to sites of operations. Another, termed as T-sheet automatically perform pricing activities. A time sheet tracking software allows for more than one type of time entry, for more than one project, and generates more than one reports for these projects. Information and data can be exported to a chosen destination. With such a tool, the business can assume numerous projects at a time and keep pace with them without encountering challenges. Seemingly, a core time billing software offer solution to project management providing invoicing services, account receivables, tracks deliverables and enters work quickly.

Make it simple for yourself. Going digital will not just make the business operations simple for your clients but will also be of huge assistance to you. If you streamline your operations by going digital in some cases, you save up time for other brain-teasing operations. Consolidating technology to already existing conventional methods means you will be in the top of your game thus; in no distant time, the benefits of digitalization would be reaped. Its time you convert those operations to tech-savvy activities to save time and cost.
