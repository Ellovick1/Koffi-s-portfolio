What is a Remote Design Sprint?

Fundamentally, a design sprint is an effortless framework that communicates sense from abstract business ideas and turns them into testable and real prototypes. The design sprint customer journey takes through a five-day course with teams meeting physically at a location. However, facilitating the process with teams not in the same physical location is considered as a Remote Design Sprint?

Are Design Sprints Important?

Yes! Fabricated under a double fulcrum of efficiency and speed rather than perfection, Design Sprints cut short months of endless debate and development, sets clear targets with reasonable level of urgency while having eyes on the goals, model flexible and adaptable frameworks, allow for a more synchronized team collaboration, inspire creativity, reduce waste and minimize risk.

Can I run Design Sprints myself?

No, Design Sprints are facilitated as a team, without which, the Sprint seizes to happen, it is vital to assemble a group from various discipline. A good design sprint has a mix of Senior Executive to call the shot, a Facilitator; to keep track of progress, Marketing Expert; to craft customer-centric messages, a Design Expert; to assist realize vision through designs, Tech Expert to decide what to build and a Financial Expert; to work out project cost and returns.

What are the Phases of a Typical Design Sprint?

The Design Sprint typically follows five phases each corresponding to a day within the duration of the sprint including; the Map, Sketch, Decide, Prototype and Test. The issues are raised, understood and mapped on Monday, possible solutions contextualized on Tuesday, while decision on the best course of action concluded on Wednesday. On Thursday, a porotype is forged and gets tested on Friday.

Are there Design Sprint Books to use as a Guide?

Yes, there are numerous Design Sprint Books which could act as a guide. However, only few are dependable. Irrespective the team size; whether start-ups or big firms, profit oriented or the nonprofits, Design Sprint Books can proffer real time answers to critical questions and offer opportunity to those seeking answers today. An ideal Sprint book to get is “The 1000 Sprint Ways”
