New York Sober living Recovery Sequencing

The New York Sorber living house places prominence on fitness, and routine exercises as well as adequate nutrition for the residents. The soundness of the mind and body foster spiritual growth. Discreting the addict or the alcoholic from drugs and alcohol is foremost, but a holistic healing process for sober living encapsulates the body and spirit alike. For this reason, the following are the sequence for a total recovery.

  • Spiritual Counselling: Advisors are on the ground to advise, counsel and guide the residents spiritually in New York sober living homes.
  • 12- Step meeting: The clients takes part in a 12- step routine meeting scheduled to provide solutions to life's issues and assist full recuperation from the ills of addiction.
  • Individual Step Work: The sober living house presents workshops on a weekly basis. The essence of which is to collaborate in recovery which will assist them through sobriety.
  • Gym Membership: Clients fitness is given pride of place at the New York sober living houses. As a facility which provides an inclusive recovery, clients are expected to acquire their gym membership. The gym membership guarantees them access to the gym 24/7.
  • Weekend Outings: The New York Sober houses believe the recovery processes should be fun-based and so at the leave of the clients, outings are scheduled. Activities including skiing and whitewater rafting are organized occasionally..
