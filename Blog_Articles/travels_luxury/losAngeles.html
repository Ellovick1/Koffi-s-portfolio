10 Best things to do in Los Angeles

Since there are numerous site attractions and activities to get engaged with, tourist and visitors seldom get confused deciding on what can pass for a memorable stay in Los Angeles. Notwithstanding the duration of your visit, whether long or short, you can make it exciting and worthwhile. Are you considering a Los Angeles trip? Alternatively, Are you already here in Los Angeles, now what next? Make your stay worth. Here are a few places to visit or exciting things to do.

1. Get thrilled on the Catalina Island
Image credit

Do you dream of having a feel of the Mediterranean in Southern California? Then it would be best if you headed a bit off the city shore. Technically, though you are still in Los Angeles County, it seems you have moved onto the coast of Greece when you make it to Catalina Island. A lot of fun things are done differently here in Catalina; notably, Avalon-the town that receives the majority of visitors. You will sight the big fishes as the fly and glimpse the people riding golf carts rather than cars. The high point of the island is the charm, and nerve relaxing ambiance which the environment provides. Your week-long vacation will never be complete without a trip to the Catalina Island.


2. Visit the Venice Beach
Image credit

Location: 1800, Front Walk, Los Angeles; the Venice Beach is has received full attention from visitors over the years. It makes a list as one the most visited sites in Los Angeles. The tantalizing sandy beach and blue waters justify the eminence and prominence bestowed on it. Usually, the beach is often lively and filled with fun activities all through. Some of which include skateboarding, joggers, rollerblades and the street shows which adorns the walkways. Most of the visitors to the beach enjoy sunbath as they get thrilled by the shaved ice and corn sold from the food stalls around the beach. The beach operates 24 hours


3. Hit the Disneyland
image credit

Located 1313 Disneyland Dr, Anaheim, CA 92802, USA; the Disneyland makes it to this list because of its cleanliness, amiable employees, and the fun factor. It is just the ideal destination for tourist. It is one of few parks that do not charge an extra fee for attraction. If you are in Los Angeles and you are considering places to visit, then hit Disneyland. This masterpiece, put together by Walt Disney, provides an atmosphere for both the young and old, as well as parents and children. The high points here include parades, colorful fireworks, and shows. Disneyland neighbor; California Adventure also has a Disney park with a collection of rides pillared on animated films, a shopping mall, dining, and the entertainment zone. It operates between the hours of 9 am and 11 pm with a fee around INR 6742


4. Wander through the Hollywood Walk of Fame
image credit

Located in North Highland Avenue and Hollywood Boulevard, the Hollywood Walk of Fame is also one of the most famous tourist locations as far as Los Angeles; the United States is concerned. If after your visit to the Hollywood Walk of Fame and your day is not met, then nothing will make your day in Los Angeles. Schedule a date and take a walk along the area to see the pink terrazzo with the famous artist enlisted in Gold. Here, you will have the opportunity to see, meet, and appreciate the several celebrities that come around for pleasure, funfair, premier, and other celebration. There is also a Chinese Theater with famous hand and footprint to see. It operates for 24 hours.


5. Relax at the Universal Studios
image credit

Location: 100 Universal City Plaza, California, 91098, USA. Universal Studios is the supreme of it all. It provides an inclusive experience and offers all-around relaxation to visitors with the feel of LA’s movie-making magic. You can embark on studio tours and get titillated by the classic film set which promises a mesmerizing experience. Films such as Harry Potter, the Simpsons, Transformer, and Jurassic Park will make your stay worthwhile. The Universal studio is a spot you would want to be as a first-time. It operates between the hours of 10 am and 6 pm


6. Sight the Hollywood Sign
image credit

You can have a glimpse of the Hollywood sign whenever you are in Les Angeles. The sign can be viewed from several locations in LA. The breezeway balconies located in Hollywood and Highland Center is one of the prominent viewing spots. On a clear day, you can have a vivid glimpse at the sign from a distance. Nevertheless, it would be best if you didn't plan on going too close to the sign. You know why? The sign is off limit and heavily guarded under security all round the clock daily. It is not open to the generality of the public.


7. Explore the Getty Center
image credit

Location: 1200, Getty Center, Los Angeles, California, 90049. The Getty Center is a vast museum which stands massively on a hilltop in the mountain plain of Santa Monica. The Getty environ is aligned with alluring works of arts, and sculptures that lead into the complex. The museum has a collection of European artworks and paintings, photography’s and decorations from previous centuries. Undoubtedly, the Getty Center is one of the foremost holidays making Centre in Los Angeles. It operates between 10 am to 5:30 pm.


8. Stop by the Griffith Observatory
image credit

Image credit Location: 4730 Crystal Springs Dr, Los Angeles, CA 90027, USA. Present in the Griffith Parks, just close to the Los Angeles’s zoo, the Griffith Observatory is home to the planetarium. Call in here to view the stars and moon directly with the aid of the Zeiss telescope. You can also observe the sun with the solar telescope. After that, hit the serene park, the zoo or the Greek theater which is located nearby. You can also trek the breathtaking views. It operates every Tuesdays to Friday within the hours of 12 pm to 10 pm while on Saturday to Sundays from 10 am to 10 pm. You will get to pay an entry fee of INR 534


9. Visit the Legoland California Resort
image credit

From downtown LA, the distance is about 1-hour 40-minute bus ride. Everything surrounding the Legoland is merely amazing, mainly for families. The resort is an ideal option when the parents intend to allow their kids to join them on vacation. There are a lot of fun-filled activities. They include twirling teacups and miniature roller coasters.

Also, Lego fanatics will have comfort strolling through the to-scale replicas of New York, New Orleans, Las Vegas, and Washington, D.C. At a price slightly higher, you can enjoy the water park. The bevies of Lego-related shopping are other options for you to hit.

Dream it, think it and make it. Starting at $95 and $89 per day for an adult and a child respectively, you can access the Legoland California Resort.


10. Make it to the Farmers Market
image credit

Woah! Are you yet to visit the farmers market? If not, you really must be missing a lot. The Farmers Market is an extensive area with stalls for restaurants, café, and food vendors. The market which was inaugurated in 1943 also has historic landmarks and suitable attraction sites. The market operates on a 24/7 basis and has in stock over 100 sellers including grocers, and ready-to-eat foods. You can call in for any delicacy; American cuisines, Asian or Latin American.
