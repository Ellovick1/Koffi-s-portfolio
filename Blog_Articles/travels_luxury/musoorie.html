A Mussoorie Visit; Inspiring Things to Do

Exclusive and classy perfectly describes the experience of a Mussoorie visit. Nicked named the “Queen of Hills,” Mussoorie is home to many awe-inspiring mélanges of nature. The breathtaking landscapes, magnificent Himalayas and alluring cuisines of the people and hotels in Mussoorie are seemingly a splendor to behold. With views from the abundant natural plain combined with the friendliness of its inhabitant, Mussoorie doubtlessly proposes an unbridled luxury and a heart-warming ambiance. It is a choice destination for travelers and has played host to visitors who troupe in from all over the globe to admire its tranquil and impeccable ambiance. So, are you on a business trip or holidays? Then, satiate your mind with the sumptuousness that Mussoorie holds by doing one or all of these things.

Don’t miss the adventurous skywalk, zip lining, and Swinging

Image source:

Sky walking is one remarkable thing to do in Mussoorie. Go up a height of about 120ft and take a walk on a 360ft long single wire rope. Sensational, isn’t it? With a harness, you will be hooked to a supporting wire, so safety is guaranteed. Alternatively, check out the Zipline. At a high end of about 330ft vertical length, hop onto a pulley and slide with a speed of about 50 km/hr to the lower end. Yet still, you can opt for the Zip Swings ; a combo of the Giant Swing and Zipline. For a Zip Swing experience, zip through to the middle of a rope and swing at a high of 80ft. All three staggering activities make a perfect thriller for a holiday.

Climb the Mountains and Rumble the caves

Image Source:

How daring are you? Are you courageous enough? Then experience the unique feel from climbing the mountains and rappelling. These two are most typical in Mussoorie. Here, abundant mountains and rocks are varying from about 50ft to 600ft. Depending on your physical capacity, mental soundness and general toughness, choose and climb a mountain. You can also rumble the caves. Just like climbing the mountains, your gut must be intact. Now go above the fears and rumble through the hidden caves of Buranskhanda. Situated 6km just before Dhanaulti; the cave is girdled with pleasing trees, alpine meadows and stunning views of the Himalayan peaks

Thrill your taste buds with palatable Delicacies

An experience of the lip-smacking cookery of the upland is phenomenal and certainly one thing to do in Mussoorie. Savor your taste buds with the culinary thrills which the several hotels in Mussoorie , cafes, and bars have to offer. Whether you are on a holiday or a business trip, call into one of the hotels for a variety of delicacy including Pan Asian, Indian, Western and Mediterranean cuisines.

Walk the Woods and Trek the Mountains

Image Source:

Mussoorie is home to alluring landscapes including the luxuriant mountains, flourishing green valleys, and thriving forests. Whoosh!!! It’s time to walk the woods and the mountains. Go towards the Happy valley from Library Head. See the clouds end and move to the Company Bagh. Trek the nature trails through the woods and glimpse the beautiful birds, flora, and fauna. For a memorable trekking experience, traverse the several Mussoorie camping and trekking options including; Nag Tibba Summit, Dodital trek, Yamunotri Saptarishi Kund, and Har ki dun trek.

Hit the Kempty Falls

The kempty falls is undoubtedly a regular call for tourist in Mussoorie. You wouldn’t want to miss the sight of the fall for nothing. The sensational gush of water from a high height, the illuminating strike of the water against the rock, the relaxing feel from the fresh air around it, and the stress winding capability are the climax of the fall. Are you already in Mussoorie or you are planning a visit, then be sure to include the Kempty Fall in your list as one fantastic place to visit.

Mussoorie is notorious for fun and adventure all the way. Obviously, aside from these few mentioned, there are many other adventures to catch. The list is just endless. So, what are you waiting for? Hit Mussoorie and make that holiday worthwhile.
